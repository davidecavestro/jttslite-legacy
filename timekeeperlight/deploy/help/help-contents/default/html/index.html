Welcome to JTTSlite 2.5 Help


What is it?

JTTSlite is just a Job Time Tracking System.

Features

With JTTSlite you can:

  • record start and end time for your activities

  • organize your activities

  • generate and print reports

  • import and export data.

Starting

This is a brief glossary to easier the usage of the entire help:

  • a workspace defines an ambit of usage of the application. In other words, you can use different workspaces to handle completely different activities.

  • a task is an activity or a job for which you ant to record your efforts. It can be decomposed in sub-tasks.

  • an action is the record of a temporal interval during which you worked to a certain task.

For example, twenty minutes ago I started an action for the “Writing down Help contents” task. This task is in the JTTSlite development workspace.



Usage

Click on the desktop icon to launch JTTSlite .

When you know you have to begin a new activity at office, add a corresponding task to the tree on the left.

Every time you work at a task, start an action to record your effort duration. Remember to stop the action when you change task or you are simply interrupted.

At the end of the day, or at the end of the week, take a look at the Daily task detail report.

You will see how you really spend your time and, at least for me, it is often different from what I thought.
